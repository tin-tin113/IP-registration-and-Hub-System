# CHMSU IP System - Certificate & Badge Guide

## Overview
The CHMSU Intellectual Property Registration and Hub System includes two key features to recognize and reward contributors: **Certificates** for approved works and **Badges** for active contributors.

---

## Certificates

### What is a Certificate?
A certificate is an official document issued when the Director approves an intellectual property application. It serves as proof of registration and recognition of your IP work.

### How to Get a Certificate

**Step 1: Submit Your IP Application**
- Login to your account
- Go to Dashboard → Submit New IP
- Fill in all required fields:
  - **IP Work Title**: A clear, descriptive title
  - **IP Type**: Choose between Copyright, Patent, or Trademark
  - **Description**: Detailed description of your work (minimum 50 characters)
  - **Supporting Document**: Upload proof/documentation (PDF, DOC, JPG, PNG, up to 5MB)
- Click "Submit Application"

**Step 2: Clerk Review**
- A Clerk will review your application for completeness
- If approved for payment: You'll move to "Payment Pending" status
- If issues found: You may be contacted or the application may be held

**Step 3: Make Payment**
- Once Clerk approves your application, make payment to CHMSU
- Submit your payment receipt through the system
- The Clerk will verify your payment

**Step 4: Director Approval**
- The Director reviews your complete application
- If approved: Your certificate is automatically generated with:
  - **Certificate Number**: CHMSU-CERT-YYYY-##### (unique identifier)
  - **Reference Number**: CHMSU-IP-YYYY-#### (for tracking)
  - Your name, IP title, and date of approval
- Certificate appears in your dashboard

**Step 5: Download Certificate**
- Go to Dashboard → View Certificates
- Your certificate displays with a QR code for verification
- Print or download as needed

### Certificate Features
- **Unique Certificate Number**: Prevents forgery
- **Reference Number**: For official tracking and reference
- **QR Code**: Scannable verification code
- **University Seal**: CHMSU official branding
- **Professional Format**: Ready for printing and framing

### Certificate Uses
- Proof of IP registration with CHMSU
- Portfolio addition for academic/professional use
- Grant applications and research credentials
- Official documentation for IP claims
- Sharing with third parties and organizations

## Badges

### What is a Badge?
Badges are achievement awards given to users whose published works gain traction in the CHMSU IP Hub. They recognize contribution and engagement in the intellectual property community.

### Badge Levels

| Badge | Views Required | Innovation Points | Color |
|-------|----------------|--------------------|-------|
| **Bronze** | 10+ views | 50 points | Bronze |
| **Silver** | 50+ views | 150 points | Silver |
| **Gold** | 100+ views | 300 points | Gold |
| **Platinum** | 250+ views | 500 points | Platinum |
| **Diamond** | 500+ views | 1,000 points | Diamond |

### How to Earn Badges

**Automatic View Tracking**
- Every time someone views your approved work in the IP Hub, a view is automatically recorded
- View counter accumulates and badge requirements are tracked

**Manual Award by Clerks**
- Clerks can manually award badges to exceptional contributors
- Basis: Community engagement, quality of work, or special recognition

### Badge Benefits
- **Recognition**: Display badges on your profile
- **Innovation Points**: Earn points toward special recognition
- **Community Standing**: Show expertise and contribution level
- **Portfolio Enhancement**: Highlight achievements

### View Count Requirements
- **Finding View Counts**: 
  - Go to Admin Panel → Manage Badges
  - See total views for each user's published works
  - Track progress toward next badge level

- **Increasing Views**:
  - Publish high-quality IP work descriptions
  - Include relevant keywords for searchability
  - Share your published works within the community
  - Ensure your work is complete and professional

### Badge Award Process (for Clerks)

**To Award a Badge:**
1. Login as Clerk
2. Go to Admin Panel
3. Select "Manage Badges"
4. Find the user to award
5. Select badge level from dropdown
6. Click "Award"
7. User receives notification and innovation points

## Application Status Flow

\`\`\`
Submitted → Clerk Review → Payment Pending → Payment Verified → Director Review → Approved
                    ↓
               (Rejected)
\`\`\`

**Status Definitions:**
- **Draft**: Application saved but not submitted
- **Submitted**: Awaiting clerk review
- **Payment Pending**: Clerk approved, awaiting payment
- **Payment Verified**: Payment confirmed by clerk
- **Approved**: Director approved, certificate generated
- **Rejected**: Application did not meet requirements

## Workflow for Different Users

### For Students/Regular Users

1. **Submit Application**
   - Prepare your IP details and supporting documents
   - Submit through "Submit New IP" form
   - Receive submission confirmation

2. **Track Status**
   - View My Applications to see current status
   - Receive updates as application progresses

3. **Payment Phase**
   - Once clerk approves, make payment
   - Submit payment receipt
   - Wait for verification

4. **Receive Certificate**
   - Director reviews and approves
   - Certificate automatically generated
   - Download from dashboard

5. **Build Badges**
   - Publish your approved work to IP Hub
   - Share with community
   - Accumulate views toward badges

### For Clerks

1. **Review Applications**
   - Go to Admin Panel → Review Applications
   - Check completeness and validity
   - Approve or hold for revision
   - Add notes for director if needed

2. **Verify Payments**
   - Go to Admin Panel → Verify Payments
   - Check payment receipts from applicants
   - Enter payment amount
   - Approve and forward to director

3. **Manage Badges**
   - Go to Admin Panel → Manage Badges
   - View user statistics and view counts
   - Award badges to deserving contributors
   - Add notes or recognition

### For Directors

1. **Approve Final Applications**
   - Go to Admin Panel → Approve Applications
   - Review clerk notes and payment verification
   - Make final approval decision
   - Add director comments
   - Certificate auto-generates

2. **View Analytics**
   - Go to Admin Panel → Analytics
   - See system statistics and trends
   - Track application progress
   - Monitor badge distribution

3. **Audit Trail**
   - Go to Admin Panel → Audit Trail
   - View complete system activity
   - Track all approvals and changes
   - Maintain records for compliance

## FAQ

**Q: How long does certificate approval take?**
A: Usually 3-5 business days after payment verification

**Q: Can I revise my application?**
A: Yes, if in draft or submitted status. Contact clerk if additional edits needed after payment.

**Q: What if my payment is rejected?**
A: Clerk will contact you with specific issues. Resubmit corrected receipt.

**Q: How many works can I publish?**
A: Unlimited! Each approved work counts toward your badges.

**Q: Can badges be revoked?**
A: No, badges are permanent once awarded. They recognize past achievement.

**Q: What do innovation points do?**
A: They contribute to your profile standing and community recognition level.

**Q: Is the certificate legally binding?**
A: It's an official CHMSU registration record. For legal IP protection, consult official IP agencies.

**Q: Can I share my certificate?**
A: Yes! Certificates are yours to share professionally and academically.
